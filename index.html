Overview

Welcome to our website dedicated to the evaluation process of Luigi-oral. This site has been curated specifically for pathologists to provide guidance and streamline their evaluation of Luigi-oral.

Luigi-Oral is an AI-based image retrieval system for oral histopathology diagnosis. The system allows users to input histopathological images and search for similar images in the database to assist observation and diagnosis. Luigi-Oral is the newest addition to Luigi system, which can be accessed in https://luigi-pathology.com/.

Detailed information on the original Luigi system is available in Cell Reports 38(9), 110424, 2022.

Requirement

  • Pathology/Oral Pathology Resident or Board-Certified Pathologist/Oral Pathologist by the time of evaluation (August 2023). An institutional email address or local certification number is required.
  • Have access to the WHO Classification of Head and Neck Tumor 4th Edition book during the evaluation.
  • Use Mac or Windows 8-11 PC with Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox installed and a mouse.
  • Connected to a stable internet connection with a minimum speed of 5 Mbps download and 2 Mbps upload during the evaluation. Internet speed can be checked at https://speed.cloudflare.com/.

Additional information

Please note that evaluators will be acknowledged in the publication but not included as authors to avoid conflicts of interest. Evaluators will be anonymized when the results are analyzed. The results will never be used to evaluate an individual's ability of pathological diagnosis (individual-level accuracy will not be shown in the paper).
